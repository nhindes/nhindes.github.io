Basil and The Isles of Spice

Nominated for Student Game of the Year 2024 | On Steam now!

Key Contributions

  • Implemented dynamic audio emitters to provide consistent volume from large sources
  • Created a system to emit particles in time with the music, bringing the world and characters to life
  • Designed and implemented 2 minigames to add essential character and flavor to the world

Crab Minigame

  • Scripted the interaction between the player and crab
  • Animated the crab using keyframes

Hide and Seek Minigame

  • Scripted the interaction between the player and Saffron

Dynamic Audio Emitters

  • Constrained an audio emitter to a given plane using linear algebra
  • Ensured scalability and ease of use by using Unity's in editor Plane Object

Particles From Sound

  • Worked with audio lead to generate the appropriate notes in time with the music
  • Connected several particle emitters to Wwise using a simple script
